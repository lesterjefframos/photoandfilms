Who am I?

I am Lester Jeff Ramos. Born in 1991 at San Juan City but I grew up in San Jose, Baggao, Cagayan. I completed my primary and secondary school at Saint Joseph College. In college, I entered the seminary in Aparri, Cagayan. I am a graduate of Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy at Thomas Aquinas Major Seminary, Lyceum of Aparri. Right after graduation, I went out of the seminary and immediately took 2-year course in Multimedia Arts at STI College Muñoz-EDSA in Quezon City and graduated in 2014.
